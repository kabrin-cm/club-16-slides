Current State

What's working today

  • Price is unbeatable. $4/week baseline. No competitor touches this.
  • Trevor Linden brand. Instant credibility with BC locals.
  • 17 locations. Covers the entire Lower Mainland.
  • She's FIT sub-brand. 6 women-only locations. No competitor has this.

  • Instagram: 14K followers, ~1,700 posts. Consistent output.
Current State

What needs to change

  • Ads going to homepage. Too many options, too many clicks. Need dedicated landing pages per offer.
  • 3-day free pass is buried. Should be the primary offer for cold traffic.
  • Tracking infrastructure unclear. Meta Pixel + Conversions API must be installed and verified. Nothing works without this.
  • No retargeting running. Website visitors, video viewers, social engagers are not being followed up. Free money left on the table.
  • Creative variety is low. Need UGC content: member testimonials, facility tours, price reaction videos.
  • No location-specific targeting. Each gym should have its own geo-targeted ad set.

Lower-Ticket Playbook

At $4/week, the strategy changes

Volume Over Qualification

Cast a wide net. The price removes the biggest objection. Don't over-target. Let Meta's algorithm find the buyers.

Speed to Conversion

Short funnels. Minimal form fields. Mobile-first. Free pass signup should take under 30 seconds.

Impulse-Friendly Framing

"Less than a coffee a day." "$4/week. That's it." "Your gym costs less than Netflix."

Social Proof at Scale

"17 locations. Thousands of members. $4/week." Numbers sell at this price point.

Retention Is the Real Game

Ads get them in the door. Experience keeps them paying. Post-signup email nurture: Day 1 welcome, Day 3 check-in, Day 7 class recommendation, Day 14 PT upsell, Day 30 celebration. LTV is months 6-36+.

Summary

The play is simple

1. Install tracking (Pixel + CAPI)

2. Build dedicated landing pages (free pass + $10/mo promo)

3. Create UGC content (3-5 Reels, iPhone quality is fine)

4. Launch 3 campaigns (Prospecting 60% / Retargeting 25% / Geo-Conquest 15%)

5. Lead with the free pass on cold traffic

6. Push urgency on warm traffic ($10/mo expires 3/31)

7. Test 4 locations first, then scale to 17

8. Track cost per new member, not just cost per lead

9. Build email nurture to maximize retention and LTV

The price advantage does the heavy lifting. The ads just need to get the price in front of the right people at the right time.
